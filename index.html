Chocolate Chip Cookie Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Butter or margarine, softened
  • 3/4 cup Sugar
  • 3/4 cup Brown sugar, packed
  • 2 Eggs, large
  • 2 teaspoon Vanilla
  • 2 1/4 cups Flour, unsifted
  • 1 teaspoon Baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon Salt
  • 1 cup Walnuts or pecans, chopped (opcional)
  • 2 cups KCC Bittersweet 60% Cacao Baking Chips

Baking Instruction

  1. Heat oven to 375
  2. Stir flour with baking soda and salt; set side.
  3. In large mixing bowl, beat butter with sugar, and brown sugar at medium speed until creamy and lightened in color
  4. Add eggs and vanilla one at a time.
    Mix on low speed until incorporated.
  5. Gradually blend dry mixture into creamed mixture.
    Stir nutes and chocolate chips
  6. Drop by tablespoon onto ungreased cookies sheets
  7. Bake for 9 to 11 minutes or until chocolate chips cookies are golden brown
  8. Enjoy!
