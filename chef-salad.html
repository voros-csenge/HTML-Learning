Chef salad

Ingredients for the salad

The proportions can be adjusted according to taste.

  • 2 heads of romaine lettuce
  • 500 g of fresh chicken breast fillet
  • 150 g fine flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 150 g breadcrumbs
  • 50 g Lovilio Grana Padano, in one piece
  • 150 g cherry tomatoes
  • sunflower oil for frying the chicken

Ingredients for the dressing

  • 1 whole egg
  • 3 egg yolks
  • 100 ml of sunflower oil
  • 100 ml extra virgin olive oil
  • 3 cloves of garlic, boiled
  • 1-2 salted and/or smoked anchovy fillets, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
  • Juice of 2 lemons
  • 60-80 ml of white wine or apple cider vinegar
  • 40-50 g Lovilio Grana Padano cheese, grated
  • salt
  • freshly ground black pepper

Peparation

  1. Heat the sunflower oil in a pan.
  2. Strip the chicken breasts, season with salt and pepper and mix thoroughly.
  3. We bread the meat in the classic way, then fry the slices in hot oil.
  4. When they are ready, let them rest on a wire rack or kitchen paper towel. While the chicken is roasting, assemble the salad.
  5. For the dressing, make a soft mayonnaise from the whole egg and the yolk with the oils. When the eggs have nicely absorbed the oils, add all the other seasonings: mustard, garlic, lemon juice, vinegar and parmesan.
  6. A good caesar dressing is neither too thick nor thin: it has a strong, spicy and salty taste. Chop the cherry tomatoes into a larger mixing bowl, and add the romaine lettuce leaves
  7. Cut strips from the cheese with a vegetable peeler, put them in the bowl and spoon some of the caesar dressing over them.
  8. Add the breaded chicken breast strips and mix loosely.
  9. Serve immediately.
